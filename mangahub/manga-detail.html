In a bid to improve their soccer standings, the Japanese Football Association contracts the mysterious coach Jinpachi Ego to assist them in winning the FIFA World Cup. Believing that Japan's problem is their lack of a true striker, he creates Blue Lock — a prison-like facility — where he selects 300 of Japan's best strikers under 18 and pits them against each other. The one who survives will become the world's greatest egotistical striker, while all others will be permanently banned from the national team.
